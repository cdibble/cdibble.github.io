Connor D. Dibble
Marine Ecology and Oceanography

Connor Dibble is a PhD candidate at the University of California, Davis' Bodega Marine Laboratory in Dr. Steven Morgan's group. He studies the role of physical oceanographic processes in shaping marine population dynamics. His work focuses on larval transport, nearshore ocean processes like flow separation and internal waves, and, most recently, spatial dynamics of marine fisheries. He also works on citizen science and communications/outreach via the California Collaborative Fisheries Research Program and the Bodega Marine Sciences Association.
